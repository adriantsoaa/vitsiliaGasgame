Let's play!

Use the keyboard arrows to move the members of your team.

The goal is from entering the playground and succeeding to get out of the other side to the finish line without being caught by the opponent team. Make care not to go along of the length Land.

The game will be over once all members of your team have each done their turn.

* Your team has 5 players and align yourself horizontally before the game starts.
