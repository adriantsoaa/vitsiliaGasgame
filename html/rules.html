Vitsilia's Rules

The framework of the game is a rectangle, subdivided into 6 squares aligned two by two. Here, two groups of the same number face each other. One of them is designated as "boka" (literally leper) or "hawk" whose role is to capture the rest of the team who are trying at all costs to enter and then exit the frame without being hit by "the hawk". The latter while remaining on the line delimiting the frame, tries to touch those who want to enter or leave.

The two groups of "Vitsilia" are the image of two opposing camps. A spirit of struggle and activism is therefore instilled. This attempt to enter or leave a protected area reflects a desire to overcome obstacles.
