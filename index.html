Have you ever heard
the "Vitsilia" word ?

Vitsilia is a mixed team Malagasy game that develops physical strength and speed in players. It is especially on the Malagasy highlands that we meet the "Vitsilia". It requires a frame drawn on the ground and can last from 20 to 30 minutes.
